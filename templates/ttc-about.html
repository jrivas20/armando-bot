The Foundation

Born From A Lifelong Obsession With Time

The Ticking Club was founded in 2012 out of a single, uncompromising conviction: that the acquisition of a fine timepiece deserves the same care, intimacy, and discretion as the horological mastery invested in the watch itself. What began as a private circle of collectors quietly trading references has become Central Florida's most trusted name in bespoke watch consultation.

Our philosophy is shaped by the watchmakers we admire — that patience, precision, and personal service are not luxuries but prerequisites. Every client is paired with a dedicated specialist. Every piece is authenticated, documented, and placed with deliberate intention. There is no showroom pressure, no fluorescent lights, no transactional haste. Only the quiet attention a connoisseur deserves.

Rooted in Central Florida and connected to a global network of makers, dealers, and collectors, we have spent over a decade refining what it means to acquire a watch the way it was always meant to be acquired — through relationship, through knowledge, and through time.

The Philosophy

Three Principles That Guide Everything We Do

Total Discretion

Our clientele includes some of the most private individuals in the region. Every consultation, transaction, and delivery is handled with the confidentiality of a trusted advisor. Your taste, your collection, and your acquisitions remain entirely your own.

Uncompromising Authenticity

Every timepiece is authenticated through a multi-point horological inspection by our certified specialists. We source only from vetted channels, document provenance rigorously, and stand behind every piece with a written authenticity guarantee.

The Client First. Always.

We do not sell inventory — we source intentions. Our specialists listen first, advise second, and never place a watch on a wrist that doesn't belong there. If the right piece isn't available today, we wait. The client, not the sale, defines success.

Our Expertise

Horological Knowledge, Human Service

Behind every consultation stands a team of certified horologists, seasoned collectors, and market strategists who have dedicated their careers to the study of fine watchmaking. We understand caliber architectures, complication lineages, and the subtle market dynamics that separate a prudent acquisition from a regrettable one.

Our mastery spans the full spectrum of haute horlogerie — from the iconic sports references that define the modern market to the rarest grand complications reserved for true connoisseurs. We speak the language of Geneva, Glashütte, and Le Sentier fluently, and we translate it into counsel that serves you.
