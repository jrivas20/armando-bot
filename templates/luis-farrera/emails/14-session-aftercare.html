Hi {{contact.first_name}},
Your tattoo is fresh — and the next few days are critical. Follow these instructions exactly. This is what separates a piece that looks incredible for years from one that fades or needs fixing.
Option 1 — Recommended
Second Skin
•  Leave it on for 4–5 days.
•  As long as the tattoo is fully covered — leave it alone.
•  Fluid inside (plasma and blood) is completely normal.
•  If it peels and exposes the tattoo → remove it completely.
Faster healing  ·  Less infection risk  ·  Low maintenance
Option 2
Traditional Healing
No Second Skin
First Nights — Very Important
•  For the first 2 nights, keep the tattoo covered with a wet paper towel.
•  Goal: keep the area hydrated while you sleep.
•  Luis will apply the first cover after your session and show you how.
Cleaning Routine
•  Wash your tattoo 2 times a day — morning and night.
•  Use liquid baby soap or tattoo-specific soap.
•  Create foam in your hands first — never apply soap directly.
•  Let it air dry. Do not rub.
Clothing & General Care
•  Wear loose clothing — friction causes irritation.
•  Avoid rubbing on the tattoo at all times.
•  Keep pets away — no touching or licking the area.
•  Avoid dirt, dust, and contaminated environments.
Cream — Very Important
First 4 Days
•  Use only Hustle Butter (provided by Luis).
•  Apply a thin layer — a very small amount.
•  Max 2–3 times per day, only if the tattoo feels dry.
More cream is NOT better. Overusing it can affect healing.
After Day 4
•  Lubriderm (blue bottle)
•  Vaseline with Aloe Vera
•  Any fragrance-free lotion

Always apply small amounts only.
Common Mistakes That Damage Your Tattoo
•  Touching it with dirty hands.
•  Scratching or picking at scabs.
•  Overwashing or over-moisturizing.
•  Using random or non-recommended products.
•  Letting it rub against dirty clothes or surfaces.
The Reality
Getting the tattoo is only half of it — how you take care of it is what really matters.
Do it right  →  heals faster, looks better, lasts longer.
Do it wrong  →  it fades, gets damaged, needs a touch-up.
