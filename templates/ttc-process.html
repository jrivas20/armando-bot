The Process

From First Inquiry To Your Wrist

A four-step ritual refined over a decade — most bespoke acquisitions complete in two to six weeks, depending on the reference and the client's timeline.

01
I
Step One

Book A Private Consultation

Your journey begins with a single, confidential form. There are no inventory pages to browse, no pressure to commit, and no public trail. Submit your inquiry and one of our senior specialists will personally respond within twenty-four hours to schedule your private intake call.

This first touchpoint is where relationship replaces transaction. We listen. We learn. We establish the foundation of trust upon which every subsequent acquisition will rest.

  • Personal response within 24 hours from a dedicated specialist
  • Private intake call scheduled at your convenience
  • Complete confidentiality from the first contact forward
  • No obligation, no pressure, no sales script
02
II
Step Two

The Discovery Session

During your private consultation, your specialist explores the dimensions that matter most — your taste, your lifestyle, your existing collection, your intended use, your budget range, and your long-term horological ambitions. This is not an interrogation; it is a conversation between connoisseurs.

By the end of this session, your specialist has a clear and nuanced portrait of the wrist for which they are curating. From this point forward, every reference they consider is filtered through an understanding as specific as it is discreet.

  • In-depth exploration of taste, lifestyle, and horological goals
  • Review of existing collection and gap analysis
  • Budget range and investment philosophy discussion
  • Curated shortlist presented — never a generic catalog
03
III
Step Three

Sourcing & Authentication

With your brief in hand, we activate our global network — authorized boutiques, independent watchmakers, private collectors, and vetted dealers from Geneva to Tokyo. Most sourcing windows close within one to two weeks, though rarer references may require patient pursuit measured in months.

Every candidate timepiece undergoes a rigorous multi-point horological inspection — caliber authentication, case and dial verification, bracelet and clasp review, and complete provenance documentation. Only pieces that pass every stage reach you.

  • Global network activation through trusted private channels
  • Certified multi-point horological authentication
  • Complete provenance and service history documentation
  • Written authenticity guarantee with every acquisition
04
IV
Step Four

White-Glove Delivery

The final act of a bespoke acquisition should feel ceremonial. Your timepiece arrives in its original presentation, accompanied by authentication documentation, service records, and warranty materials — all hand-delivered, insured, and scheduled to your preference. Where appropriate, we accompany the delivery personally.

The relationship does not end at handover. Your specialist remains your concierge for sizing, servicing, insurance coordination, and future acquisitions — for as long as you wear the watch.

  • Insured, hand-delivered, and scheduled at your convenience
  • Complete documentation package with every timepiece
  • Ongoing concierge for service, sizing, and insurance
  • Lifetime relationship with your dedicated specialist
