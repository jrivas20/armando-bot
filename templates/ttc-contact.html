Frequently Considered

Questions From The Discerning

What brands do you specialize in?

+

Our specialists curate exclusively from the most prestigious maisons of Haute Horlogerie — Rolex, Patek Philippe, Audemars Piguet, Richard Mille, Vacheron Constantin, A. Lange & Söhne, Jaeger-LeCoultre, IWC Schaffhausen, Panerai, and other connoisseur-tier brands. We do not source mass-market timepieces.

Is the consultation free?

+

Yes. Your initial consultation with a senior horological specialist is entirely complimentary and carries no obligation. We believe a relationship built on trust must begin without a price tag attached.

How do you authenticate timepieces?

+

Every reference undergoes a rigorous multi-point authentication protocol — movement examination, serial verification, case and dial analysis, papers and provenance review. Our master horologists hold over a decade of experience in identifying both authentic masterpieces and the most sophisticated counterfeits on the market.

Can you source a specific reference that's hard to find?

+

Bespoke sourcing is the cornerstone of our atelier. Through our discreet relationships with international dealers, private collectors, and waitlist holders, we have placed references that clients had been pursuing for years. Tell us what you covet — we will find it.

Do you handle pre-owned watches?

+

Absolutely. The vintage and pre-owned market houses some of the most coveted masterpieces in horology. Every pre-owned timepiece we place is authenticated, condition-graded, and accompanied by complete provenance documentation.

What is the typical acquisition timeline?

+

Timeline depends on the rarity of the reference. In-stock pieces from our network can be delivered within days. Bespoke acquisitions of waitlisted or vintage references may take weeks to months — but we work with the patience worthy of pursuing a true masterpiece.

Do you offer trade-in services?

+

Yes. We facilitate trade-in valuations for clients seeking to refine their collection or fund a new acquisition. Each trade is appraised at fair market value by our senior specialists with full transparency throughout the process.

Is my information kept confidential?

+

Absolute discretion is non-negotiable. Your identity, holdings, communications, and acquisitions remain entirely confidential. We never share client information with third parties — a principle we hold with the same reverence as the timepieces we represent.
