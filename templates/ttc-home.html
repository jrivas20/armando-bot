The Atelier

Not Just A Watch.
A Statement Of Mastery.

Founded on a single, uncompromising principle — every timepiece we place must be flawless in provenance, condition, and significance. The Ticking Club operates as an invitation-only horological consultancy serving collectors, connoisseurs, and discerning gentlemen across Central Florida and beyond.

Our specialists hold over a decade of experience navigating the rarefied world of Haute Horlogerie. From sourcing impossible-to-find Patek Philippe complications to authenticating vintage Rolex references, we deliver a bespoke service worthy of the legacies we represent.
