Why only tattoo?

One Niche.
Done

We could sell this system to restaurants, law firms, real estate agents. The infrastructure works for all of them. We don't.

Tattoo is different. The buying decision is emotional, visual, and trust-heavy. The client journey goes from scroll → admire → DM → book → sit for hours. That's a completely different funnel than a dentist or a gym.

We built everything — every ad angle, every follow-up sequence, every intake question — for that exact journey. Not adapted from something else. Built from scratch for tattoo.
