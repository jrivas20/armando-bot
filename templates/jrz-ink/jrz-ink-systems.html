Just Answers.

Everything you want to know before applying. If it's not here, ask us directly.

Do you work with any tattoo artist? +

No. We're selective on purpose. We work with artists who have a clear style, a portfolio worth showing, and are serious about running a real business. If you're still figuring out your direction, we're probably not the right fit — yet. Apply and we'll be honest with you in the first call.

How fast will I see results? +

Most artists see their first leads within 48–72 hours of launch. Significant booking momentum typically builds in weeks 2–4 as the pixel learns and the follow-up sequences kick in. Month 2 is usually when the system hits its stride. We don't promise overnight miracles — we build something that runs for years.

What exactly is included in the booking system? +

A premium multi-step intake form that qualifies leads before they hit your calendar. Every submission auto-creates a contact in your CRM, tags them by style request and budget, fires an SMS confirmation, and drops into the pipeline. You receive a real-time notification the moment someone books. That's it. That's your only job.

Do you run the ads or do I manage them? +

We run everything. Ad account setup, campaign builds, creative strategy, audience research, pixel events, A/B testing, and weekly optimization. Your job is to show up for sessions. We handle every ad-related decision. You get a performance dashboard so you always know what's happening without having to ask.

What if I already have a website? +

We'll audit it. If it converts and ranks, we'll connect the booking system to it. If it doesn't — and most tattoo artist sites don't — we'll rebuild it from scratch as part of your System or Elite plan. A website that doesn't book people isn't an asset. It's a missed opportunity.

Is there a long-term contract? +

No. Every plan is month-to-month. We don't lock you in because we don't need to. The results do that. We ask for 90 days to let the system build momentum — that's not a contract, that's just how long it takes for paid ads to optimize and the pipeline to fill properly.

Who pays for the ad spend? +

You do — directly to Meta. We never touch your ad budget. We recommend starting with $300–$600/month in ad spend for the Launch plan and $600–$1,500 for System. These go directly to your Meta Ads account. Our fee is separate and covers the management, strategy, and system.

What makes you different from a general marketing agency? +

We don't take any other kind of client. Not gyms, not restaurants, not chiropractors. Only tattoo artists. That means every campaign we've tested, every funnel we've built, every follow-up sequence we've written was for someone exactly like you. The learning curve is already done. You get a system that was already proven before you arrived.
