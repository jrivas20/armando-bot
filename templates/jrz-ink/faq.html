Questions.
Answered.

Ask anything before you apply. We'd rather you come in informed than waste 30 minutes on a call covering the basics.

Do you work with any tattoo artist or studio? +

No. We're selective on purpose. We work with solo tattoo artists and studios who have a clear style, a portfolio worth showing, and are serious about running a real business. Whether you have one chair or ten — if you're ready for a system and not just ads, apply. If you're still figuring out your direction, we'll be honest with you on the first call.

How fast will I see results? +

Most artists see their first leads within 48–72 hours of launch. Significant booking momentum typically builds in weeks 2–4 as the pixel learns and follow-up sequences kick in. Month 2 is usually when the system hits its stride.

What's included in the booking system? +

A premium multi-step intake form that qualifies leads before they hit your calendar. Every submission auto-creates a CRM contact, tags them by style and budget, fires an SMS confirmation, and drops into the pipeline. You get one real-time notification when someone books.

Do you run the ads or do I manage them? +

We run everything. Ad account setup, campaign builds, creative strategy, audience research, pixel events, A/B testing, weekly optimization. Your only job is to show up for sessions.

What if I already have a website? +

We'll audit it. If it converts and ranks, we connect the booking system to it. If it doesn't — and most tattoo artist sites don't — we rebuild it as part of your plan. A website that doesn't book people is a missed opportunity, not an asset.

Is there a contract? +

No. Month-to-month. We don't lock you in because we don't need to. The results do that. We ask for 90 days to let the system build proper momentum — that's not a contract, that's just how long ads take to optimize and a pipeline to fill.

What does it actually cost? +

Three numbers: $1,000 one-time setup fee to build everything (website, ads, CRM, automations, booking system). $500/month flat management fee to keep the system running and optimized. Then 10–12% booking performance tied to what your dedicated booking manager drives — this is negotiable based on your studio's revenue. If we don't fill your calendar, your performance share is zero. We win when you win.

Who pays for the ad spend? +

You do — directly to Meta. We never touch your budget. We recommend $300–$600/month to start. That goes straight to your Meta Ads account. Our $500/month management fee is separate and covers strategy, optimization, and the full system. Ad spend is on top of that and goes directly to the platform.

What makes you different from a general agency? +

We don't take any other kind of client. Not gyms. Not restaurants. Not chiropractors. Tattoo artists and studios — that's all we do. Every campaign we've tested, every funnel we've built, every follow-up sequence was written for someone exactly like you. We also pair every client with a dedicated booking manager who fits your specific setup — solo artist or multi-chair studio. The learning curve is already done. You get a system that was proven before you arrived.
