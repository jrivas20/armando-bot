Our Story

A Houston Glazing Company
Built on Precision

AMS Glazing was founded in Houston, Texas with one mission: deliver commercial and industrial glazing at a level of precision and professionalism the market demanded but rarely got. What started as a focused commercial glazing operation has grown into one of Greater Houston's most trusted names in storefront systems, curtain walls, and specialty glass.

Over 15 years, we've completed projects across the full spectrum of commercial construction — retail centers, medical facilities, office towers, industrial plants, and government buildings. Every project gets the same level of technical attention and on-site discipline that built our reputation.

We're Houston-based. We know the city's codes, the Gulf Coast climate demands, and what commercial buildings in this market need to perform long-term. No shortcuts. No substandard materials.
