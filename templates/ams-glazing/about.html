Our Mission & Values

What Drives Every Project We Take On

Precision First

Commercial glazing is an engineering discipline. We measure twice, cut once, and install to spec every single time — no field-fit workarounds.

Contractor Partnership

We work the way GCs and project managers need us to — on schedule, on spec, communicating proactively when conditions change in the field.

Code Compliance

Every AMS installation meets or exceeds Houston and Texas building codes. We stay current with IBC updates and Gulf Coast windstorm requirements.

Rapid Response

Emergencies don't wait for business hours. AMS provides 24/7 emergency response for commercial glass failures throughout Greater Houston.

Quality Materials

We source glass and framing systems from proven manufacturers. No cost-cutting on materials — the long-term performance of every installation depends on it.

Local Expertise

Houston-based and Houston-focused. We understand the climate, the codes, the contractors, and what buildings in this market need to perform.
