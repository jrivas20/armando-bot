Our Story

A Houston Glazing Company Built on Precision

AMS Glazing was founded in Houston, Texas with a single mission: deliver commercial and industrial glazing at a level of precision and professionalism that the market demanded but rarely got. What started as a focused commercial glazing operation has grown into one of Greater Houston's most trusted names in storefront systems, curtain walls, and specialty glass installations.

Over 15 years, AMS Glazing has completed projects across the full spectrum of commercial construction — retail centers, medical facilities, office towers, industrial plants, and government buildings. Every project gets the same level of technical attention and on-site discipline that built our reputation in the Houston market.

We are a Houston-based glazing contractor. We know the city's building codes, we understand the Gulf Coast climate demands, and we show up to every job ready to perform. No shortcuts. No substandard materials. No excuses.
