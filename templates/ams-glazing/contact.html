FAQ

Common Questions
Before You Call

How fast can you respond to an emergency glass situation?

For emergency glass repair in Houston, AMS Glazing aims to have a crew on-site within 2–4 hours of your call, depending on location and traffic. We provide board-up and temporary glazing immediately, with permanent replacement scheduled as soon as materials are confirmed. Call (281) 691-2338 directly for the fastest response.

Do you work with general contractors and property managers?

Yes — a significant portion of our work comes through GCs, property management companies, and facility managers. We provide compliant submittals, coordinate with your project schedule, and can work as a subcontractor on projects of any size. Subcontractor pre-qualification packages are available on request.

How do I get an accurate quote for commercial window replacement?

The most accurate quotes come after a site visit. We'll take field measurements, assess existing frame conditions, identify any structural or code issues, and specify exact product options. You can request an on-site survey through the form above or by calling us directly. For straightforward replacements with known dimensions, a preliminary estimate can sometimes be provided over the phone.

What areas of Houston do you serve?

We serve commercial clients throughout the Greater Houston metro area including Houston proper, Katy, Sugar Land, The Woodlands, Pearland, Cypress, Pasadena, Baytown, Missouri City, League City, Friendswood, and Conroe. For projects outside this radius, contact us — we evaluate large-scope work on a case-by-case basis.

Are you licensed and insured for commercial glazing work in Texas?

Yes. AMS Glazing is a licensed glazing contractor under the Texas Department of Licensing and Regulation (TDLR) and carries full general liability and workers' compensation insurance. We can provide certificates of insurance and license verification upon request prior to project award.

Do you offer financing or payment terms for large projects?

For larger commercial and industrial projects, we can discuss phased payment schedules tied to project milestones. Standard terms are outlined in our contracts. Please mention this during your consultation and we will work with you on a structure that fits your project budget and timeline.
