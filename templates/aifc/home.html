About the Conference

THIS ISN'T A
TYPICAL
CONFERENCE.

The Albania International Fire Conference (AIFC) is the first event of its kind in the country — designed to accelerate firefighter development through real-world, scenario-based training.

Built through a partnership between Fire Rescue Group (USA) and AFPRA (Albania), the event connects international expertise with local fire service growth.
