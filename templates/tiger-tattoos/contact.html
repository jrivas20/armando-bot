Common Questions

BEFORE YOU
VISIT.

Do I need an appointment to get a tattoo or piercing?
No appointment needed — both studios are walk-in friendly Thursday through Sunday. For larger or more complex tattoo projects, calling ahead helps us set aside enough time for your piece. For piercings, walk-in is always fine.
What should I bring to my first appointment?
Valid government-issued photo ID (you must be 18+ for tattoos and most piercings without parental consent). For minors, a legal guardian must bring their own ID and remain present. Come hydrated, eat a solid meal beforehand, and wear clothing that gives easy access to the area being worked on.
How much does a tattoo cost?
Pricing depends on size, placement, detail, and time. Small pieces start at a shop minimum. Large custom work is quoted per piece. The best way to get an accurate number is to walk in with your reference and have a quick consultation — it's free and you'll leave with a real number.
What forms of payment do you accept?
We accept cash and all major credit/debit cards. Deposits may be required for large custom projects to hold your artist's time. Walk-in and same-day services are typically pay-at-completion.
Is parking available at both studios?
Yes — both locations have free parking. Kissimmee (4145 W Vine St) has a strip mall lot. Deltona (1382 Howland Blvd Suite 8) is in a plaza with ample parking directly out front.
Can I get a tattoo if I'm pregnant or nursing?
We follow industry-standard guidelines and do not tattoo clients who are pregnant or actively nursing. This is for your safety and the safety of your child. Please consult your healthcare provider and wait until you're cleared before booking.
