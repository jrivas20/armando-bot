Common Questions

TATTOO FAQ.

Do I need an appointment?
Walk-ins are always welcome at both our Kissimmee and Deltona locations. For larger, more complex pieces, calling ahead is recommended to secure time with a specific artist.
How much does a tattoo cost?
Pricing depends on size, placement, complexity, and style. Small pieces start from a shop minimum. Large custom work and sleeves are priced by session. We'll give you an honest quote during your free consultation — no surprises.
How do I prepare for my tattoo session?
Eat a full meal beforehand, stay hydrated, wear comfortable clothing that gives easy access to the tattoo area, and avoid alcohol for 24 hours before your appointment. Arrive rested and ready.
How long does a tattoo take to heal?
Surface healing typically takes 2–3 weeks. Full skin healing can take 3–4 months. We provide detailed aftercare instructions after every session — follow them and your tattoo will look incredible long-term.
Can you cover an old tattoo?
Absolutely. Cover-ups are one of our specialties. Bring in a photo of your existing tattoo and we'll assess what's possible. In most cases, we can design something that completely transforms it — no laser needed.
Do you offer touch-ups?
Yes. We stand behind our work. Touch-ups for work done at our studio are handled professionally — reach out to the location where you got your piece and we'll take care of you.
