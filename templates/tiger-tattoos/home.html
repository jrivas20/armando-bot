Special Requirements
INFANT &
MINOR PIERCINGS

We proudly offer safe, professional piercings for infants and minors. Our licensed piercers follow strict safety protocols with your child's comfort as the priority. A welcoming, inclusive environment for every family.

Parent or legal guardian must be present throughout the entire appointment
Valid government-issued ID required for the parent or guardian
Minor's birth certificate or school ID may be required
Consent form must be signed before any piercing is performed
Notary services available at both Kissimmee and Deltona locations
Age Requirements
Infants
Ear lobes only. Parent/guardian consent required. We recommend waiting until infant is at least 2 months old and in good health.
Minors (Under 18)
Most piercings available with verified parental consent and a valid signed form. Some restrictions may apply based on age and piercing type.
Notary Services
Need a notarized consent form? We assist with notary services at both our Kissimmee and Deltona studio locations. Ask our team upon arrival.
