JR Paver Sealing was founded right here in Orlando, Florida by a craftsman who believed that every driveway, patio, and pool deck deserved professional-grade care. What started as a local paver sealing operation grew into Central Florida's most trusted full-service paver contractor.

From simple paver sealing near me searches that brought in their first clients, to large-scale paver installation projects for Orlando's newest communities, JR Paver Sealing has never once compromised on quality.

We are licensed, insured, and locally owned. When you call JR Paver Sealing, you get a real Orlando team — not a franchise, not a subcontractor, not a national chain. People who live here, work here, and stand behind every square foot we touch.
