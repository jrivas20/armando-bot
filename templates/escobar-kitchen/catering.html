FAQs

Catering Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your catering order.

How far in advance should I book catering?

We require a minimum of 48 hours advance notice for catering orders. For large events of 100 or more guests, we recommend booking at least 1 week in advance to ensure full availability and menu customization.

What is the minimum order for catering?

Our platters start from $180. The minimum order amount varies by event size and menu selection. Contact us directly for a custom quote tailored to your specific needs.

Do you deliver or set up?

We offer both drop-off and pickup options for all catering orders. Setup service is available for larger events. Please mention your preference when submitting your catering quote request.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the Orlando metro area from our 3 locations in Hunters Creek, Lake Nona, and Downtown Orlando. Contact us to confirm coverage for your specific location or event venue.
