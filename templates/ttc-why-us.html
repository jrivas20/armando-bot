The Six Pillars

Six Reasons Discerning Collectors Choose The Ticking Club

I

Private Access & Allocation

Skip the public waitlists and dealer politics. Our decade-deep relationships with authorized boutiques, independent watchmakers, and private collectors unlock references that never reach the open market. Priority access is not a promise — it is a standing arrangement.

II

Authentication Guaranteed

Every timepiece passes a certified horological inspection — caliber, case, dial, bracelet, and provenance. You receive a documented authentication report with each acquisition, and our written guarantee stands behind the piece for as long as you own it.

III

Your Dedicated Specialist

One specialist. Yours. They learn your taste, your collection, your lifestyle, and your long-term vision. When a reference you've mentioned becomes available — or when the market moves against a watch on your wishlist — you hear about it first, from someone who remembers.

IV

Investment Intelligence

A fine watch is both a possession and a position. We provide market data, value trajectory analysis, and portfolio-minded counsel so your collection appreciates as intentionally as it was assembled. Passion and prudence are not opposites — they are partners.

V

Central Florida's Network

Rooted in the region and connected to a global network of makers, dealers, and collectors from Geneva to Tokyo, we bring global reach with local relationships. Our clients know us by name — and we know theirs.

VI

White-Glove Concierge

From private intake to insured, white-glove delivery, every touchpoint is choreographed. Sizing, documentation, insurance coordination, service scheduling — handled. You wear the watch. We handle everything else.
