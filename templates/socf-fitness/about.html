SOCF Fitness was born from a simple idea: the best gym in Orlando shouldn't just be about equipment — it should be about people. We built a space where intensity meets community, where every class is coached by someone who genuinely cares about your performance.

Nine years in, every step has been guided by our members. From our first functional fitness classes to expanding into HYROX training, Competitors, and Kids Fit — we now serve 400+ athletes across 4 programs. The mission has never changed.
